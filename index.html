We are Providing Services Since 1978 With Pride.

Welcome to Kaffa, where tradition meets excellence in every cup. Since our establishment in 1978, we have been dedicated to crafting the finest coffee experiences for our customers.
Named after the legendary birthplace of coffee, Kaffa embodies a rich heritage and a deep passion for quality. For over four decades, Kaffa has been a trusted name for coffee enthusiasts, offering premium blends sourced from the finest beans around the world. Our commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing ensures that every sip not only delights your senses but also supports the farmers and communities who grow our beans.
At Kaffa, we believe that coffee is more than just a beverage—it’s a journey of flavor, culture, and connection. Whether you’re savoring our signature brews at home or in one of our welcoming spaces, you’ll always find the perfect blend of taste and tradition. Join us in celebrating the art of coffee, one cup at a time.
